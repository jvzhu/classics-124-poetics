## Quick-reference: the throughline in one sentence

Defenses of transgressive representation — across two thousand years and two unconnected traditions — consistently locate their justification in some external frame (improving fiction, diagnostic philosophy, doctrinal rule, trained reader, converging judges), and in every case the frame does more than defend the content: it produces the very moral and gender categories it claims only to explain. See `cross-synthesis.md` for the full argument and its one clean exception (the Chongzhen commentator).
